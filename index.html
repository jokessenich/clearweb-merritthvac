About the shop

Local crew. Honest work.

Merritt Repair is a family-run HVAC shop based in Hinesville, working homes, rental properties, and small commercial sites across four coastal Georgia counties.

Our approach is simple: diagnose the real problem, explain it plainly, and quote a fair price before we pick up a tool. If that sounds obvious, ask around about how often it actually happens.

Diagnosis, quote, approval — then we turn a wrench. No inflated "emergency" fees. You see the price before work starts.
Licensed, insured, and bonded in Georgia. State license available on request. Every visit is documented.
Most service calls answered the same day. Typical on-site arrival: around 45 minutes from the initial call.
Written warranty on every repair and install. If something fails that we just fixed, we come back on our dime.
